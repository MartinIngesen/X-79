X-yachts X-79 resources

X-yachts X-79 is a sailing yachts made in the 1980s. Here are some files/resources on it I've collected over the years.

Resources

  • Assembly/build manual
  • German X79 Class association rules from 2002 (In English, source: x79.de)
  • Some commercial brochures: first second
  • X-79 specification february 1991
  • Sample polar diagram.
Most content is originally mirrored from www.x79.dk. As of December 2015 x79.dk appears to be dead. Please mirror this content to keep the files available. Yachts last longer than web sites.
