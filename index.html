X-yachts X-79 resources

X-yachts X-79 is a sailing yachts made in the 1980s. Here are some files/resources on it I've collected over the years.

Update 2018-05-18: I've added a PDF version of the typewritten trim guide by Niels Jeppesen. The text (OCR-ed) is in ocr-source.de.md. Proper transcription and translation into an english version would be nice!
